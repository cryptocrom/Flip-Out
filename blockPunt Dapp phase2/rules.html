blockPunt

How to Begin

1. Deposit some funds
2. Select the game you would like to play
3. Have a punt

Please note that all bets require oracle data,
the cost of the query (approx. 4 finney) will be deducted from the losing party's account

All bet amounts and deposit amounts are in units of finney (0.001 ether)

You can withdraw your betting funds at any time from the main page.

A classic coin flip game

1. Select how much you want to bet
2. Select either heads or tails
3. Flip out!!!

A winning bet will payout 2 times the bet amount

Guess a number, roll the dice

1. Select how much you want to bet
2. Select any number between 1 and 6, inclusively
3. Roll out!!!

A winning bet will payout 6 times the bet amount

High card draw against the house

1. Select how much you want to bet
2. Dealer draws a card
3. Decide if you think you're card will be higher or lower
(Ace is low, King is high)
4. Draw Out!!!

Winning bets will vary depending on the dealer card showing, see payout table below
